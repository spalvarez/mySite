Pomodoro Clock

What is a pomodoro clock?

A pomodoro clock is a way to get yourself moving with work or an activity. Here's how it works: You set the session, which how long you want to work for. This is traditionally 25 minutes. Once that time is up, you rest and relax for a period of time, typically 5 minutes. Rinse and repeat, that's it! The idea is it's much easier to get started on something when you know you'll only do it for a short period until taking a break! Use the clock below to give it a try!
